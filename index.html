Olive Oil

Today I will discuss the pros and cons of olive oil. Olive oil is a healthy oil used in cooking. As someone who has used olive oil for years, I will talk about the pros and cons of using it.

Pros


  • Healthier than traditional oils like canola and vegetable oil.
  • Has a lower smoke point than other oils. Might not be best for deep-frying.

Cons


  • Olive oil has a nice taste and gives things like salads and ice cream more flavour.
  • It has a noticeable flavour, unlike other oils which don't impart flavour onto the food they cook.
  • Great alternative to things like peanut oil, which some people might be allergic to.
  • It is pricier than other oils. Getting olive oil is expensive.
